Love Yourself: Answer

Love Yourself: Answer (LOVE YOURSELF Áµê 'Answer') is the second compilation album by BTS. It was released on August 24, 2018 with "IDOL" serving as the album's title track. The album contains the tracks from their two previous mini albums, seven new songs and three new remixes.

The album debuted at #1 on Billboard 200, becoming BTS's second #1 on that chart.

 The album was the best-selling album of 2018 in South Korea and second best-selling album of 2018 in the world.

 This album is currently the fourth best-selling album on South Korea of all time.

 As 2020, the album has sold more than two and a half million copies.
