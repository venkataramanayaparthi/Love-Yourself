Love Yourself: Tear

Love Yourself: Tear (LOVE YOURSELF è½‰ 'Tear') is the third studio album by BTS. It was released on May 18, 2018 with "FAKE LOVE" serving as the album's title track.

The album debuted at #1 on Billboard 200, becoming BTS' first #1 on that chart.

 The album was a nominee for the GRAMMYs "Best Recording Package".

 The album was the second best-selling album of 2018 in South Korea. It is currently the nineth best-selling album on South Korea of all time..

 As 2020, the album has sold more than two million copies.
